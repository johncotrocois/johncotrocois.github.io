Stand Up Against Bullying

A short film

Synopsis

A struggling stand-up comedian rushes to a gig expecting a paycheck, only to find himself at a somber anti-bullying fundraiser where he's completely out of place. As he navigates an increasingly bizarre backstage — including a confrontational sock-based expression artist — and reconnects with an old friend, he learns he's in fact not getting paid and that the event's story isn't quite what the organizer makes it out to be. When a chaotic altercation spills onto the stage, he's forced into the spotlight, bruised and disheveled. Afterward, in a quiet moment outside, the night takes an unexpected turn as his friend shares life-changing news that has him choosing to show up in a way he hadn't for a year.

The Creators

John Cotrocois

Writer & Performer

John Cotrocois is a Greek-Canadian comedian and storyteller living in Montreal, Canada. He has appeared on various Just For Laughs segments, gone viral for his small Greek village content, has opened for Iliza in Athens, and regularly performs at Montreal's most popular comedy venues.

John is working hard on developing a series based on his life, a small snippet of which is being portrayed in this year's submission to the JFL Shorts competition. He also has high hopes to publish a memoir and, one day, have his very own special.
